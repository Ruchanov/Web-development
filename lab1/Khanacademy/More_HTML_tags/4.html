Pizza Margarita

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: _
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1.5
Cups Sugar 2 Teaspoons
Salt 1 Teaspoons
Dry yeast 3/4 Teaspoon
Olive oil 3.5 Teaspoons
Warm water 0.5 Cups
Tomato paste 85 Grams
Clove Mozzarella 170 grams
Tomato 1
Spices To taste

Step 1: Mix flour, 1 teaspoon sugar, 0.5 teaspoons salt, yeast, 1.5 teaspoons olive oil, warm water.

Step 2: Form the dough into a ball and cover it with a film. Leave for 1 hour.

Step 3:After an hour, the dough will rise 2-3 times, it will be lush and breathable.

Step 4:Sprinkle the tabletop with flour and lay out the dough, roll it into a layer.

Step 5:At this time, mix the tomato paste, remaining olive oil, sugar, salt, ground pepper and crushed garlic.

Step 6:Spread the sauce over the entire surface of the dough, leaving 1 cm from each edge.

Step 7:Cut the tomato into slices, put it, and then the mozzarella slices and greens on the dough, pepper a little.

Step 8:Bake the pizza in a preheated 180 degree oven for about 20 minutes. Serve the pizza hot.

Milanese de pollo

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: _
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 4
Pieces Eggs 2
Pieces Garlic 1
Clove Onion 1
Piece Flour 2 tablespoons
Parsley 1 tablespoon
Mozzarella 4 slices
Spices To taste

Step 1: Put eggs, flour, onion in a blender bowl (you can cut it into halves), a tablespoon of parsley, garlic, spices. Beat for a couple of minutes until smooth.

Step 2: In a separate bowl, pour the breadcrumbs so that they are ready for breading. Chicken breasts can be beaten off if they are thick. Put the pan to heat up and pour vegetable oil. Dip each breast into the egg mixture, then into breadcrumbs

Step 3: Roll the chicken on both sides and put it in a frying pan to fry until golden brown. Fry for 7-10 minutes on each side.

Step 4: Put a piece of mozzarella on the ready-made hot chicken so that the cheese melts. Serve the finished milanese de pollo with a light vegetable salad. Enjoy your meal!

Source: Povar.ru
